❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I update my restaurant menu?▼
Go to Menu Items in the sidebar. You can add, edit, or remove items, update prices, and toggle availability. Changes are reflected immediately to customers.
Why are my orders not appearing?▼
Ensure your restaurant status is set to "Open" in Settings. Also check the Orders page and clear your browser cache. If issues persist, contact support.
How does the AI combo recommendation work?▼
Our AI analyzes your historical order data using Market Basket Analysis to find items that are frequently ordered together. These become suggested combos shown to customers.
How do I process a refund?▼
Go to Orders, find the relevant order, click View Details, and use the "Issue Refund" option. Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days to the original payment method.
What are the subscription plans?▼
RestaurantAI offers Starter (free), Professional (₹2999/mo), and Enterprise (custom) plans. Contact our sales team for enterprise pricing and custom integrations.
